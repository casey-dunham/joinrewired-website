Frequently Asked Questions

How long are the daily sessions?

Each session takes about 10 minutes. This includes a personalized lesson, an interactive exercise, and your daily micro-action.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage your subscription in the app by going to Settings → Manage Subscription. You can also manage it through your Apple ID settings under Subscriptions.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete Account in the app. This will permanently remove all your data from our servers. This action cannot be undone.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! Your daily sessions and progress are stored locally, so you can complete sessions without an internet connection. Your data will sync when you're back online.

What if I miss a day?

That's okay! Rewired is designed to meet you where you are. You can pick up right where you left off. Consistency matters more than perfection.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your journal entries and chat conversations are private. We use encryption to protect your data, and chat messages are deleted after insights are extracted. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How does the AI coach work?

The AI coach is trained to provide supportive, evidence-based guidance tailored to your specific beliefs and progress. It's not a replacement for professional therapy, but a tool to help you apply cognitive techniques in your daily life.
